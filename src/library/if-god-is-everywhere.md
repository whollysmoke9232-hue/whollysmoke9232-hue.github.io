> ***Where can I go from Your Spirit? Or where can I flee from Your presence?***  
*—Psalm 139:7*

---

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then I do not merely walk with Him — I exist *within* Him.</span>
<span class="indent">I breathe in His presence, and He breathes through mine.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then "as far as the east is from the west" is still within His embrace.</span>
<span class="indent">There is no exile from His love, no distance from His gaze.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then He sees me as I am, as I was, and as I will be — *all at once.*</span>
<span class="indent">Not in sequence, but in fullness.</span>
<span class="indent">Not in progress, but in completion.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then He knows everything that can be known about me — and infinitely more.</span>
<span class="indent">The things I hide, the things I fear, the things I don't yet understand about myself</span>
<span class="indent">He holds them all without flinching.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then what I call an obstacle is both there and not there.</span>
<span class="indent">It is real in my time, but resolved in His.</span>
<span class="indent">It is a shadow cast by a light I haven't yet seen.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then He is not "working on me." He has already finished.</span>
<span class="indent">I am the one catching up to the masterpiece.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then all of my current and future problems have already been solved.</span>
<span class="indent">All of my questions already answered.</span>
<span class="indent">I am not waiting for clarity — I am walking toward it.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then maybe when I get to Heaven,</span>
<span class="indent">I'll realize I've been there all along.</span>
<span class="indent">That eternity was never a destination, but a dimension I was always tethered to.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then even my sins and weaknesses will become stained-glass windows</span>
<span class="indent">Broken pieces made beautiful by the light of His grace.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then the power that created galaxies and raised Jesus from the grave *dwells in me.*</span>
<span class="indent">Not metaphorically. Literally.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then I am connected to everything — because everything exists within Him.</span>
<span class="indent">Every atom, every angel, every moment, every miracle.</span>

If God is everywhere,  
<span class="indent">Then nothing is impossible for me.</span>
<span class="indent">Because nothing is impossible for Him.</span>
<span class="indent">And He is not just beside me — He is in me.</span>
<span class="indent">And I am in Him.</span>
