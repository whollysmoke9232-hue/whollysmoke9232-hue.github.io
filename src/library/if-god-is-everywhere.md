## Key Scripture

> *“Where can I go from Your Spirit?*  
> *Or where can I flee from Your presence?”*  
> **—Psalm 139:7 (NIV)**

---

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then I do not merely walk with Him—I exist within Him.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I breathe in His presence, and He breathes through mine.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then “as far as the east is from the west” is still within His embrace.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There is no exile from His love, no distance from His gaze.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then He sees me as I am, as I was, and as I will be—all at once.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not in sequence, but in fullness.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not in progress, but in completion.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then He knows everything that can be known about me—and infinitely more.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The things I hide, the things I fear, the things I don’t yet understand about myself  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;He holds them all without flinching.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then what I call an obstacle is both there and not there.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is real in my time, but resolved in His.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It is a shadow cast by a light I haven’t yet seen.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then He is not “working on me.” He has already finished.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I am the one catching up to the masterpiece.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then all of my current and future problems have already been solved.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All of my questions already answered.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I am not waiting for clarity—I am walking toward it.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then maybe when I get to Heaven, &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I’ll realize I’ve been there all along.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;That eternity was never a destination, but a dimension I was always tethered to.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then even my sins and weaknesses will become stained-glass windows  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Broken pieces made beautiful by the light of His grace.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then the power that created galaxies and raised Jesus from the grave dwells in me.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Not metaphorically. Literally.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then I am connected to everything—because everything exists within Him.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Every atom, every angel, every moment, every miracle.

If God is everywhere,  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Then nothing is impossible for me. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Because nothing is impossible for Him.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And He is not just beside me—He is in me.  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;And I am in Him.

---

## Prayer

> Lord, expand my awareness of Your nearness. Help me to rest in the truth that I am never outside Your presence, never beyond Your reach, and never unseen by Your love. Teach me to live from that reality, with trust, humility, and awe.  
> Amen.
