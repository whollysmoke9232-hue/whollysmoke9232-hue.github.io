## About the Book

Walking with God is rarely dramatic, but always transformative. It is formed in daily dependence, repeated surrender, and quiet obedience through both clarity and confusion.

*Hold My Hand: Walking With God* gathers seven reflections that call believers into a more intentional, trusting walk with Christ. Each chapter invites the heart to move from self-reliance toward companionship with the One who leads, sustains, and refines.
