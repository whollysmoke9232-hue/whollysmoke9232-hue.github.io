## About the Book

*My Story: From Brokenness to Grace* is a three-act journey through fracture, collapse, and awakening. These chapters follow a real path through addiction, arrest, surrender, confinement, and the slow rediscovery of God in places once filled with shame.

This book is for readers who need honest testimony, resilient hope, and reminders that grace is strongest where we are weakest.
