## About the Book

*Letters From Confinement* gathers reflections, prayers, and meditations written through a season of confinement.

The movement of the book is deliberate: from collapse, to being alone with God, to honest prayer, confession, awakening, and finally new sight. Each section traces what happens when suffering, honesty, and grace are allowed to speak without interruption.

This edition is arranged for both chapter-by-chapter reading and continuous reading mode.
