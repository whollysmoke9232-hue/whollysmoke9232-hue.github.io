Before time began, God already knew.

Before creation unfolded, the story was complete in His mind.

This section explores the eternal nature of God’s knowledge, presence, and purpose—the margin before the beginning where nothing was uncertain and nothing was accidental.

Here, we learn to trust the God who sees the whole tapestry when we can only see a single thread.
