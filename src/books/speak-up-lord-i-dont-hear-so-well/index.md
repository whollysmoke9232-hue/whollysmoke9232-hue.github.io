## About the Book

Many believers long to hear God clearly, yet often feel uncertain, distracted, or spiritually dull. *Speak Up Lord: I Don't Hear So Well* gathers seven meditations that help retrain the heart toward attentiveness.

From stillness and surrender to scripture and perseverance, these chapters invite a quieter posture before God and a deeper confidence that He is still speaking.
