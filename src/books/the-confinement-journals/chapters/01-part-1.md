The first entries are written from the underside of collapse. Silence was not chosen here—it was imposed. But in the silence, something unexpected began to happen.

The noise of a former life—its distractions, its busyness, its endless noise—fell away all at once. What remained was a question, and a Presence willing to answer it.

These pages are where it began.
