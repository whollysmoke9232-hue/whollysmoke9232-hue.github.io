Once still, something woke up. A hunger. A direction.

These entries mark the turn from passive waiting to active seeking—not for change in circumstances, but for the face of God Himself. The discovery that God wants to be desired, not merely consulted, changed everything about how prayer felt.

The seeking that begins here is not desperate. It is expectant.
