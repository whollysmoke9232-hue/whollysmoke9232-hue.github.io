## About the Book

*Standing In Awe* gathers thirteen reflections written in seasons of prayer, struggle, and rediscovery. These chapters move from the gift of the present moment into deeper trust, clearer sight, and a steadier confidence in God's character.

This edition is arranged for both chapter-by-chapter reading and continuous reading mode.
