## About the Book

Pain has a way of narrowing our world. Questions get louder. Silence feels longer. And the heart can wonder whether God is still near.

*When Life Hurts: Finding Purpose in the Pain* gathers seven meditations written for those difficult places where grief, uncertainty, and weakness press in. These chapters do not minimize suffering; they bring it honestly before God and seek His voice in the middle of it.

From tears to endurance, from unanswered questions to steadfast trust, this book points to the God who meets us in affliction and forms us there.
