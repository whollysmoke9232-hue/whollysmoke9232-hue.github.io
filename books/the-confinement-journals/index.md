## About the Book

*The Confinement Journals: Finding God in the Silence* gathers twenty pieces written during an extended season of confinement—reflections, prayers, meditations, and poems that trace what happens when silence becomes the only available language.

The movement of the book is deliberate: from entering the silence, to learning to be still, to the hunger of seeking, to awakening, and finally to the unshakeable grip of trust. Each section marks a stage not of escape, but of encounter.

This edition is arranged for both chapter-by-chapter reading and continuous reading mode.
