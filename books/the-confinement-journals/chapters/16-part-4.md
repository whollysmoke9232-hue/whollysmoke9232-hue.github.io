Seeking gives way to finding. Not all at once—never all at once—but in glimpses.

These pages document the awakening: the growing awareness of a God who is vast, near, and entirely capable of being worshipped from inside a cell. The smallness of the circumstances became, somehow, the stage for the largeness of God.

Here, transformation begins to take shape.
