There is a difference between being quiet and being still. Quiet can be forced. Still must be discovered.

These pieces trace the slow, sometimes painful discovery that the striving we bring to God is often the very thing standing between us and Him. The harder we push to fix ourselves, the further we move from the One who does the fixing.

Somewhere in the silence, the striving ran out. And that was the beginning.
