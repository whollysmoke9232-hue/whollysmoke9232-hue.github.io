Grace in the Margins

You're Not Alone

You are not being evaluated here.

If pornography or sexual compulsion has been part of your story, this page exists to say one simple thing: you are not alone.

This is not a program. Not a formula. Not a demand for immediate change.

It is a gathering of writings shaped by lived experience — offered as companionship for different seasons of struggle and recovery.

I am not writing as a victor standing on the other side, but as someone who has spent a long time in the wilderness and learned a few reliable paths — a fellow traveler who knows where the ground drops off.

Take your time. There is no rush here.

You do not need to choose the right place to begin. Start wherever feels closest to home.
