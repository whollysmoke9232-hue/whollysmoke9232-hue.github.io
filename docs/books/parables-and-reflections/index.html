About the Book

Jesus often taught in parables, drawing eternal truth from ordinary moments. Parables and Reflections follows that same movement with five meditations that invite readers to see God's hand in unexpected places.

From grass and feasts to names and roadblocks, these chapters call the heart to attention, humility, and trust, offering practical insight for everyday discipleship.
